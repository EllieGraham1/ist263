Yogurt Parfait

Recipe
Prep Time Ingredients Optional Ingredients
5 minutes Granola
Yogurt
Berries		 Chocolate Chips
Honey
Peanut Butter
  1. Grab your favorite cup of yogurt. I happen to love the chobani greek yogurts
  2. Pour some granola into the yogurt cup. I use the Nature Valley protein + to make sure I get protein in for the day
  3. Sprinkle some berries on top

Optional:

  1. Drizzle honey around the top
  2. Sprinkle in some chocolate chips
  3. Add a spoonful of peanut butter to add some extra protein! I love the Justin's Maple Almond Butter for this!

Breakfast Sandwich

Recipie
Prep Time Ingredients Optional Ingredients
15 minutes Bagel
Avocado
Pre-cooked Bacon
Eggs		 Sliced Tomatoes
Butter
  1. Start by putting some oil on a pan and heating it up
  2. While you're waiting for the pan to heat up, grab out the rest of your ingredients
  3. Crack an egg onto the pan
  4. Put your bagel in the Toaster for 4-5 minutes
  5. While waiting for your bagel to toast, check back on the egg, mix it up if you want it scrambled
  6. Heat up 2 pieces of pre-cooked bacon in the microwave. Take it out and let it cool for a minute
  7. By now, your bagel has popped up. Take it out and slice and spread avocado on each side
  8. Take the bacon and place on one side of the bagel
  9. Make sure your egg is ready and place it ontop of the bacon.
  10. Place the other bagel side on top, and you're done!

Optional:

  1. Add a slice of tomato into the sandwich
  2. Spread butter onto each side of the bagel before putting the avocado on
