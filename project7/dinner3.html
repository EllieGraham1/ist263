Pasta Bake

Recipe
Prep & Cook Time Ingredients Optional Ingredients
45 minutes Butter or Oil
Penne
Pasta Sauce
Cheese
Spinach or Other Leafy Greens		 Shredded Chicken
  1. Cook pasta until soft
  2. While pasta is cooking, butter or oil up an oven safe deep pan
  3. Smooth pasta sauce and place shredded cheese on the bottom of the pan
  4. Once the pasta is done, layer it ontop the the cheese and sauce at the bottom of the pan
  5. Layer your leafy greens ontop of the pasta. Then keep layering as desired until the pan is filled
  6. Bake in oven at 400 degrees for 15 minutes

Optional:

  1. Shred up skinless chicken breasts. Put them in the bake pan underneath the pasta

Leftover Taco Night

Prep Time Ingredients Optional Ingredients

Hard taco shells or Soft taco shells
Rice
Beans		 Anything else in your fridge
  1. Place the chicken strips on a pan and cook in the oven for 20-25
  2. Start cooking the rice and beans in separate pots, or use easy frozen heat up packages
  3. While those are cooking, heating up the taco chells in the oven for about 5 minutes
  4. Chop up vegetables you want in your taco
  5. Take out the shells and see if the rice and beans are ready
  6. Take out chicken when its ready
  7. Start lining your taco shell with ingredients. I prefer to do leafy greens on the bottom, then meat, then rice and beans, then vegetable, cheese, and sauce toppings. Have fun!

Optional:

  1. Add any vegetables or other leftover ingredients you have from your fridge or freezer into the tacos if you would like
